What is the World of Warcraft?

World of Warcraft (WoW) is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed by Blizzard Entertainment. Set in the fantasy world of Azeroth, players create characters from different races and classes, exploring vast landscapes, completing quests, fighting monsters, and engaging in player-vs-player battles. The game features an evolving story, dungeons, raids, and a deep progression system with leveling, skills, and gear upgrades. Since its 2004 release, WoW has remained one of the most popular MMORPGs, regularly expanding with new content and updates. The top 3 popular expansions for most of the gamers are The Burning Crusade, Cataclysm & Shadowlands.

The Burning Crusade

World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade is the first expansion for WoW, released in 2007. It introduces the shattered world of Outland, where players battle the demonic Burning Legion and their allies. The expansion raises the level cap from 60 to 70 and adds two new playable races: Blood Elves for the Horde and Draenei for the Alliance. It also introduces flying mounts, new dungeons, raids, and the Arena PvP system. Players explore zones like Hellfire Peninsula and Shadowmoon Valley, facing powerful enemies, including Illidan Stormrage, Kael’thas Sunstrider, and Kil’jaeden, in an effort to stop the Legion’s invasion.

Cataclysm

World of Warcraft: Cataclysm is the third expansion for WoW, released in 2010. It dramatically reshapes Azeroth after the return of the corrupted Dragon Aspect, Deathwing, who unleashes a devastating catastrophe known as the Shattering. The expansion raises the level cap to 85 and introduces two new playable races: Goblins for the Horde and Worgen for the Alliance. Classic zones are revamped, and new areas like Mount Hyjal and Deepholm are added. Cataclysm also brings new dungeons, raids, rated battlegrounds, and the ability for players to use flying mounts in the old world. The expansion focuses on stopping Deathwing and his destructive forces.

Shadowlands

World of Warcraft: Shadowlands is the eighth expansion for WoW, released in 2020. It takes players to the Shadowlands, the afterlife of Azeroth, after Sylvanas Windrunner shatters the barrier between life and death. The expansion introduces Covenants—four factions that players align with for unique abilities and rewards. It also adds the Torghast dungeon, a roguelike-style experience, and revamps the leveling system, reducing the max level to 60. Players face threats like the Jailer and uncover the mysteries of the afterlife while restoring balance to the Shadowlands.
